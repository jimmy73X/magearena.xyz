Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 30, 2023

Mage Arena ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Mage Arena. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, magearena.xyz, and our Steam game, Mage Arena. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, participate in a contest or promotion, make a purchase, communicate with us via third-party platforms, or otherwise interact with us.

a. Personal Information

We may collect personal information you provide, such as:

  • Your username or gaming alias
  • Email address
  • Steam account information (when you connect through Steam)
  • Payment information (processed by Steam and other secure payment processors)
  • Voice data when using our voice-controlled features

b. Gameplay Information

We automatically collect certain information about your gameplay and interaction with our services:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • Session data and gameplay statistics
  • Preferences and settings
  • IP address and device information
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop new features and gameplay elements
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Facilitate contests, sweepstakes, and promotions
  • Provide voice recognition services for spell casting

3. Voice Data Collection

Mage Arena features voice-controlled spells which require processing of your voice commands:

  • Voice data is processed in real-time for spell recognition
  • We do not store raw voice recordings on our servers
  • Voice processing may utilize third-party services with their own privacy policies
  • You can disable voice features in the game settings
  • Background noise may be captured during voice processing but is not stored

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information about you as follows:

  • With vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf
  • In response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with any applicable law, regulation, or legal process

  • In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company
  • With your consent or at your direction

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites, products, or services, including:

  • Steam Platform (Valve Corporation)
  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Voice recognition services

Information collected by third parties is governed by their privacy practices. We encourage you to learn about the privacy practices of those third parties.

6. Data Retention

We store the information we collect about you for as long as is necessary for the purpose(s) for which we originally collected it, or for other legitimate business purposes, including to meet our legal, regulatory, or other compliance obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Rectify or update inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@magearena.xyz.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 (or other age as required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that we may have collected personal information from a child, please contact us at privacy@magearena.xyz.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our website or sending you a notification).
