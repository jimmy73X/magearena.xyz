🎤 Voice Recognition Tips

Maximize your spellcasting efficiency with these voice recognition best practices.

Pro Tip: Practice in a quiet environment before joining competitive matches

1

Speak clearly and don't rush

The game needs to recognize each word clearly for accurate spell casting.

2

Optimal microphone placement

Keep your microphone 6-12 inches from your mouth for best results.

3

Practice makes perfect

Practice spell names in the tutorial before jumping into PvP matches.

4

Troubleshooting

If spells aren't working, reset voice settings in the menu.

5

Background noise

Background noise can interfere - use push-to-talk if needed.
